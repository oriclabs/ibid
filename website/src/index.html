Why Rust/WebAssembly?

Citation files can contain thousands of entries in complex formats (BibTeX, RIS, XML). Ibid uses a Rust engine compiled to WebAssembly to parse and serialize these formats directly in your browser — no server roundtrips, no data leaving your machine.

Import/Export Engine BibTeX, RIS, EndNote XML, MEDLINE, CSV — parsed in Rust/WASM. 1,000 entries in under 100ms.
100% Local Everything runs in your browser's sandbox. Your citations never touch a server — not ours, not anyone's.
JS Fallback If WASM can't load, JavaScript fallback parsers handle BibTeX, RIS, and CSL-JSON seamlessly.
