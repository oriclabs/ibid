Welcome to Ibid

Privacy-first citation manager. Here's how to get started.

1

Click the Ibid icon on any page

Navigate to a webpage, article, or journal and click the Ibid icon in your toolbar. Metadata fills automatically.

2

Choose your citation style

Pick from 74 styles (APA, MLA, Chicago, IEEE, Vancouver, and more). Search by name or field.

3

Copy or save to your library

Copy the formatted citation to your clipboard, or click "Add to Project" to save it in your library for later.

Pro tips

DOI Have a DOI? Paste it in the identifier field and click Enhance to auto-fill all fields from CrossRef.
Ctrl+Shift+C Keyboard shortcut to open the cite popup instantly.
Import Already have citations? Open the Library side panel and drag & drop your .bib or .ris files.

Scholarly API access (optional)

Allow Ibid to fetch metadata from arXiv, DOI.org, and other scholarly APIs for better citation accuracy. No data is collected.
